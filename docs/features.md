# Voice2Web Features & Workflow

This document explains the core modules of the Voice2Web platform.

## 1. Business Onboarding Module

The onboarding process is designed to be frictionless for non-technical users.

*   **Step 1: Registration** - Users sign up with a phone number or email.
*   **Step 2: Voice Input** - Owners describe their business naturally. *Example: "I own a grocery store in Patna. We sell milk, bread... we open at 8 AM."*
*   **Step 3: AI Extraction** - The AI extracts structured data (Name, Hours, Location, Products).
*   **Step 4: Confirm/Correct** - A human-in-the-loop step where owners confirm the AI's understanding before generation.
*   **Step 5: Website Generation** - A fully SEO-optimized, schema-markup-enabled website is automatically created.
*   **Step 6: Personalization & Verification** - Theme changes, OTP/GPS verification.

## 2. Trust & Verification Layer

To prevent spam and build consumer trust:
*   **Identity Checks**: OTP + GPS pin-drop + optional government registration (e.g., Udyam in India).
*   **Verified Badges**: Displayed on verified business profiles.
*   **Community Reporting**: Consumers can flag suspicious listings.

## 3. Consumer Discovery Module

*   **Location-based Search**: Discover businesses nearby.
*   **Direct Communication**: Contact via phone or WhatsApp. The platform does NOT act as a middleman for transactions.

## 4. Business Management Module

*   **Voice-Based Updates**: Owners can say "We are closed today" to instantly update their site.
*   **Simple Analytics**: Plain-language insights into website traffic.

## 5. Monetization Strategy

The platform **never charges commissions**. Revenue is generated through:
*   **Freemium Tiers**: Basic sites are free. Custom domains and premium themes are paid.
*   **Value-Added Services**: WhatsApp broadcast tools, marketing materials.
*   **B2B/B2G Partnerships**: Licensing to NGOs and government bodies for digital inclusion drives.
